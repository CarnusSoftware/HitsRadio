John Clothier

What can we say about JC!? ...well he started on the radio many years ago on hospital radio at Yeovil on Radio Camelot one night a week then started a second night at Dorchester at Ridgeway Radio. Then moved away to Corfu and got some proper jobs on radio doing afternoon shows for Island Radio Aqualand Radio Aktina FM and was first voice on FM and internet for Gaga Radio! And they paid me! I was the only English person on Greek Radio for many years ......hence my presenting greek radio like you to keep things moving more music less chatting! I then set up my own internet station Jayceesounds and had 7 presenting shows on there one being Gay Wilson yes GW from down under it was me who pushed her!! Well moved back to UK and have been with Abbey 104 a community radio station in Sherborne Dorset after 4 years I fancied a change and chose Hits Radio Live UK so here I am!! The Midweek Mashup goes out Wednesday night at 8.00pm til 11.00pm Live from my studio and repeated Fridays at 12.00 noon. I hope you listen into the show and enjoy it as much as I do!...Proper Job!
