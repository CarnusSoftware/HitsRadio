Kev Parker

Hailing from Exmouth in the heart of beautiful East Devon, our very own KP (Kev Parker) brings his unique style of presenting to the Hits Radio Station twice weekly. On Tuesdays he hosts the 80's Flashback from 10pm and on Thursdays the Mixed Bag show, also from 10pm. KP is 48, been married for 20 years and has presented shows on various stations since 2012. He recently moved into community radio , presenting drive time on his local station. He even once won an award for sexiest voice! Which to this day he still finds hilarious. KP will play you anything from 50's rock 'n roll to the latest big sellers,(just don't ask for any Country & Western - he's not a fan!) and is a specialist at hunting down those long forgotten tracks and those tunes you just don't hear anymore. Always up for a laugh (usually at GW's expense!) he has a wicked sense of humour and presents his shows "off the cuff" with no set playlist, just a great working knowledge of what to play and when to play it.
